Full Skill Description
Sometimes my teenagers are away from home. When that happens I have nobody around to tell me just how terrible everything is. Some unfortunate people don't ever have a teenager around. How can they possibly know that life is terrible? The Teenage Son skill can help. 

Example Phrases

Alexa, ask Teenage Son if he wants to see a movie.
Alexa, tell Teenage Son go to bed.
